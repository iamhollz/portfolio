about

Bonjour! I am holly - aka hollz!- I categorize myself as an energetic designer who listens and shapes the best result for the client all while having fun. Simple, clean, fresh designs can make my day! I have been living the designer's life for 10+ years. Starting my career in 2009 with Mobile Web design for 5 years and worked my way to a pharmaceutical advertising agency for 2 years. In April 2016 I completed a Front-End coding course with General Assembly. I wanted to see how a designer's visuals are translated into reality. That knowledge gives me the ability to design more efficiently even to this day. My current position as an Interactive Designer within a small boutique agency in Shelton, CT for 4 years.  Now I am ready to conquer more!  With my current position and my last position (pharmaceutical agency) at the client's request, you will not see that body of work represented in my portfolio publicly.
